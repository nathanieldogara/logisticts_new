About LAY Logistics

LAY Logistics is a transporting company that deals with conveying goods in large quantities from one destination to another. They are one of the major and reliable transporters in Kaduna. They cover the 36 states of the federation and deliver goods within the stipulated time frame. Some of the goods they transport all kinds of goods wihin the country. It is important to note LAY Logistics is a registered company and therefore make it convenient for their business partners to run business with them.
